Why Use Thymeleaf?

  • Natural templating - HTML that can be directly viewed in browsers

  • Modern and actively maintained

Form Tips

  • All fields are required
  • ID should be a unique identifier
  • Price should be a number (e.g., 19.99)
  • Click submit to process the form
  • Form uses Thymeleaf object binding
